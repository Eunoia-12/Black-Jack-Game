Blackjack Game

Blackjack is a popular card game played between one or more players and a dealer. The objective is to beat the dealer by having a hand value closer to 21 without exceeding it.

Card values: Number cards are worth their face value, face cards (Kings, Queens, Jacks) are worth 10, and Aces can be worth 1.

Since this is a simple project the computer makes for the dealer and randomly hands out 2 numbers

The goal is to get a sum of 21. You win the game! Get a chip points of 100

If not your out of the game! You lose 10 chip points

Click on the start game button to start a new game

The new card gives you a new card till you either win or lose the game.!
